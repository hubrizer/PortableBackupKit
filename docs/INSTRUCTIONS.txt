Adjusting Retention
-------------------
Open **backup.ps1** and change:

    [int] $RetentionDays = 30

to any integer. Snapshots older than that many days are deleted each run.

Changing Schedule Later
-----------------------
Run **setup.ps1** again; it overwrites the Task Scheduler trigger and
backup.ps1 but keeps existing credentials.
